PROFILE

I'm a film editor, critic, and screenwriter based in Burbank, California, having recently graduated Summa Cum Laude with a B.S. in Media Production, Management, and Technology from the University of Florida. My technical background spans commercial work for brands like Sony Pictures and ESPN, and I specialize in post-production workflows that prioritize narrative clarity over flash. I recently served as post-production supervisor, director, and lead editor for the short film Keira & Cole, where I managed a $20,000 budget and implemented a live-tracking production database to ensure zero schedule slippage.

I bring a unique critical perspective to the editing room. As a Rotten Tomatoes-accredited critic and 2026 Sundance Film Festival press delegate, I maintain a routine of watching at least one new movie daily. This encyclopedic knowledge of film history allows me to edit with a deep understanding of story structure and audience expectation.
